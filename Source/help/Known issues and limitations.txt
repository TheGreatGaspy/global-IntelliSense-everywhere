Very limited testing.

If you want to write "formula1" but have previously created a dictionary entry that starts with "formula" and its the first entry, it will be triggered.
You can prevent this by using formula, then e.g. left, then right, and then press 1.


EN: If there are hits in your word list, it will appear with suggestions and may obscure important areas (for example, your favorite editor's word list).
Then it is helpful to make the position of the word list appear context-specific (for experts). You have to do a little bit in the source code.
